# WiFi Won't Connect

## Problem
Computer can't find or connect to WiFi network.

## Solution
1. Go to Settings > Network & Internet > WiFi
2. Toggle WiFi off, wait 10 seconds, toggle back on
3. Look for your network SSID in the list
4. Click it and enter password
5. If network doesn't appear, restart router (unplug 30 seconds)
6. If still not working, forget the network and reconnect

## Prevention
Update WiFi drivers monthly from manufacturer website.
