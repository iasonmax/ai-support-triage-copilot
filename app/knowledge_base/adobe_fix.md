# Adobe Won't Open – Troubleshooting Guide

## Problem
Adobe reader fail to launch. Adobe reader 
will not open, is frozen, or is unresponsive on Windows.

## Solution
1. Open Windows Task Manager (Ctrl+Shift+Esc)
2. Look for any processes named "Adobe", "Photoshop", "Illustrator", etc.
3. Select each Adobe process and click "End Task"
4. Wait 10 seconds
5. Relaunch the Adobe application

## Why This Works
Adobe processes sometimes hang due to cache corruption or resource conflicts. Killing all instances forces a clean restart.

## Prevention
- Keep Adobe updated to the latest version
- Close all Adobe apps before shutting down Windows
- Restart your computer weekly
