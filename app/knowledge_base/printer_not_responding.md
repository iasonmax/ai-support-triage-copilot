# Printer Not Responding

## Problem
Printer is offline or not responding to print jobs.

## Solution
1. Open Settings > Devices > Printers & Scanners
2. Find your printer and click it
3. Click "Remove device"
4. Turn off the printer (power button)
5. Wait 30 seconds
6. Turn it back on
7. Go to Settings > Add device > select printer
8. Install drivers if prompted

## Prevention
Keep printer firmware updated monthly.
